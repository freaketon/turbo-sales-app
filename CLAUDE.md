# Turbo Sales App

## Skills

- social-media-growth-report: Generate a comprehensive, interactive Instagram growth report for any creator. Takes a creator's social media handle and produces a full HTML report with audience analysis, content strategy, trend intelligence, newsjacking engine, hook creator, and roadmap to 1M followers.
  TRIGGER when: user asks to generate a social media growth report, Instagram growth report, creator growth analysis, or mentions "Turbo report" / "growth report" for a creator/handle.
  DO NOT TRIGGER when: user asks about general social media advice without specifying a creator, or asks about unrelated analytics.
